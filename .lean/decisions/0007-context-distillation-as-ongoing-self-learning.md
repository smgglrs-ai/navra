lean-learn should support periodic distillation/compression/decay of accumulated knowledge to keep context efficient. The user proposed (MSG 596): 'Is it something we could run from time to time to distill/compress/decay information and optimize context? Is it some kind of self-learning?' This means lean-learn is not just extraction from sessions but also ongoing maintenance: merging redundant memories, archiving stale items, compressing verbose entries.
