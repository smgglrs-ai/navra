---
title: Session selection by quality not just recency
date: 2026-06-18
status: accepted
source: session 941834
---

When lean-learn processes sessions, it should select by quality (size, content richness) not just recency. The user said (MSG 1206): 'Is it most recent or also most meaningful? I created and exited quickly a few sessions which have not much. Maybe trim the sessions list to remove the ones too small. We may also have old but valuable content in older sessions, so age might not be enough.' Small sessions should be filtered out. Already-processed sessions should be tracked to avoid re-reading.
